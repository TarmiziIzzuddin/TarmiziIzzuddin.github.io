<div style="text-align: justify">Dr. Tarmizi Bin Ahmad Izzuddin delivered a captivating talk titled "AI Foundation Models and Its Applications: Unlocking The Potentials" at the Centre For Advance Analytics, Multimedia University (MMU) on November 20th, 2024. The talk delved into the fundamental concepts of foundation models, exploring their key characteristics, training methodologies, and diverse applications. Dr. Tarmizi shed light on the powerful technique of Self-Supervised Learning (SSL) and its significant role in natural language processing (NLP) and computer vision tasks. By highlighting the potential and limitations of SSL, the talk provided a comprehensive overview of the current state and future directions of foundation models in the field of artificial intelligence. </div>
